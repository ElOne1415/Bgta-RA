Bogotá Verde AR

Explore, learn and take care of Bogotá’s public parks through Augmented Reality.

How to use it

  1. Scan the QR code to open the WebAR experience.
  2. Allow camera access.
  3. Point your phone at the Hiro marker.
  4. Navigate through the educational sections.

Contents: parks, environmental care, public space rules, safety and mini quiz.
